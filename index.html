About Us

We're so glad you've taken the time to browse through our travel guides. The AP Travel Team was started by a group of 3 undergraduate students at University of Michigan (Go Blue!) who had a passion for travel. However, we quickly realized it was difficult to know what to do in each city and existing solutions were underwhelming.

We decided to build a guide dedicated to letting others know the best places to visit in cities we are familiar with. Every place on this list has been tried and tested by our very own team! We are constantly hiring new team members in other cities to expand our city guides. Look out for Boston, Washington DC, and Miami to be added in late 2015. Thanks again for visiting our site and we hope you spread the word about our guides. Happy Travels!
